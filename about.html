About Manler:

Manler is a three piece psychadelic rock band from eastern PA. Manler is an amalgamation of all three of it's members favorite musical cliches. Manler will funk you up. Manler will also rock, prog, jazz, and metal you up. Manler does not take any prisoners. Manler does enjoy Kenny G though. Manler wants you do be a loose cannon. Listen to Manler. Manler knows.

Meet Manler!

Drewsef

Drew don't know what to put here. The man is only able to play six stringed instruments; any more strings gives him a kiniption. SIX IS ENOUGH. Why do even need more? There's only twelve notes on the whole thing, what are you gonna do add another few? Ridiculous. Drew enjoys a good riff and a nice glass of single malt scotch. He also enjoys: sick delay units, a good fuzz, headbands, psychadelic garb, tapestries, a good rug to put his pedalboard on, band memes, gee-tars, jamming. Some of the musicians he enjoys: Zappa, Hendrix, Trey from Phish, Keef Richahds, da Beatles, PARK LIFE, various sad bois throughout late 90's to early 00's history, Stu.
